ABOUT

A stream featuring everything I made in my 3D design classes! I took 3D Design I & II as a high school elective and was introduced to a wide range of crafts. I learned to make things such as wooden spoons, ceramic cups, paper mache monsters, and much more. These classes were the beginning of my crafting journey and have continued to influence what I do with my free time to this day.
