ABOUT

A stream featuring my clay crafts! I originally started out clay in a classroom setting learning cearmics. Afterwards, without a kiln at home, I moved to a smaller scale and started making things using polymer clay instead. Over the years, I have dabbled in a lot of different clay crafts ranging from making small objects, fusion creations with other materials, figures, and clay painting.
